24/7 Artists Open Verse Challenge 2025

Official Rules

“The Drop” Challenge

Official Rules – 247 Artists Inc.

  1. Eligibility

    “The Drop” Challenge (the “Contest”) is sponsored by 24/7 Artists Inc. (“Sponsor,” “24/7 Artists,” “we,” or “us”). The Contest is open only to legal residents of the fifty (50) United States and the District of Columbia who are at least eighteen (18) years old and the age of majority in their state or jurisdiction of residence at the time of entry.

Employees, officers, and directors of Sponsor, its parents, subsidiaries, affiliates, advertising and promotion agencies, and their immediate family members (parents, children, siblings, spouse) and members of the same household are not eligible to enter or win.

The Contest is void where prohibited or restricted by law. All applicable federal, state, and local laws and regulations apply.

  1. Contest Period

    The Contest begins and ends on the dates and times described on the official contest page for The Drop (the “Contest Page”) (the “Contest Period”). Sponsor may, at its discretion, designate specific phases or rounds of judging with separate timelines. Any such timing details will be described on the Contest Page and are incorporated into these Official Rules.

  2. How to Enter

    To enter the Contest during the Contest Period, follow the steps described on the Contest Page. These may include, for example:

  • Accessing the official beat, stems, or other materials provided for The Drop (the “Challenge Materials”), if applicable.

  • Creating an original performance and/or recording (for example, a verse, song, or remix) using the Challenge Materials in accordance with the instructions on the Contest Page (your “Track” or “Submission”).

  • Uploading or posting your Submission on a designated platform and/or completing an entry form, including any required hashtags, tags, or links, as described on the Contest Page.

You must own or control all rights in and to any original lyrics, melodies, vocals, performances, and other elements you contribute to the Submission. No uncleared samples, unauthorized interpolations, or materials you do not have rights to use are allowed. Submissions that violate third-party rights are not eligible.

If your Submission involves multiple contributors (for example, a band, group, producer and artist, or other collaborators), you must designate one person as the “Group Representative.” The Group Representative will be considered the entrant, will be responsible for submitting the entry, and, if applicable, will be the prize recipient. The Group Representative will be solely responsible for distributing any prize among all group members and collaborators. Sponsor will not be responsible for any disputes among group members or collaborators.

By submitting an entry, you confirm that:

  • You and all collaborators consent to entry in the Contest;

  • You have the authority to submit the Submission and grant the rights set out in these Official Rules; and

  • Your entry complies with these Official Rules and any instructions on the Contest Page.

Proof of posting or submission does not constitute proof of receipt by Sponsor. Sponsor is not responsible for late, lost, incomplete, ineligible, or misdirected entries.

  1. Submission Guidelines and Conditions

By participating in the Contest, you agree that your Submission will:

  • Comply with any content or community guidelines described on the Contest Page or on Sponsor’s website;

  • Not infringe any copyright, trademark, privacy, publicity, or other intellectual property or proprietary rights of any person or entity;

  • Not contain defamatory, obscene, pornographic, violent, or otherwise offensive material;

  • Not promote hate, discrimination, or harm against any group or individual based on race, ethnicity, religion, gender, sexual orientation, disability, or any other protected characteristic;

  • Not be primarily generated by artificial intelligence tools, unless the Contest Page expressly permits it;

  • Not contain unlawful, fraudulent, or misleading content; and

  • Not be submitted or generated through automated means, bots, scripts, or similar methods.

Sponsor may disqualify, edit, or remove any Submission that, in its sole discretion, does not comply with these guidelines or otherwise violates these Official Rules.

Entrants understand that other entrants may submit content that is similar in concept, idea, or style to their own Submission. Entrants waive any claim against Sponsor or its affiliates arising from any such similarities.

Sponsor reserves the right, in its sole discretion, to disqualify any entrant or Submission, or to cancel, suspend, or modify the Contest, if it believes an entrant is tampering with the operation of the Contest, acting in violation of these Official Rules or applicable law, or undermining the integrity or legitimate operation of the Contest in any way.

By entering the Contest, you represent and warrant that:

  • You are the sole owner of, or have obtained all necessary rights, permissions, and licenses in and to your Submission and all elements contained in it, including any performances, recordings, compositions, and artwork;

  • In the case of a group or joint Submission, the Group Representative has obtained all necessary rights and permissions from all contributors and collaborators to submit the Submission and enter the Contest;

  • Your Submission does not infringe or violate any rights of any third party and will not give rise to any claims of invasion of privacy or publicity or any other claims;

  • Your Submission complies with all applicable laws and platform terms of use; and

  • You agree to be bound by these Official Rules and the decisions of Sponsor and the judges, which are final and binding in all matters relating to the Contest.

  • Operating, administering, and promoting the Contest;

Sponsor is not obligated to use any Submission. To the extent permitted by law, you agree to waive any moral rights or similar rights you may have in the Submission, to the extent necessary for Sponsor to exercise the rights granted above.

  1. Judging and Winner Selection

Eligible Submissions will be reviewed and judged by a panel selected by Sponsor (the “Judging Panel”), which may include members of the 24/7 Artists team and/or invited industry professionals.

Submissions may be evaluated based on criteria such as, without limitation:

  • Creativity and originality;

  • Performance quality (including vocal, lyrical, musical, or overall performance energy);

  • Use of the Challenge Materials and fit with The Drop concept; and

  • Overall impact and potential for release, promotion, or further development.

The number of winners, the structure of any judging rounds, and any additional judging criteria will be described on the Contest Page. In the event of a tie, the Submission receiving the higher score in one or more criteria selected by Sponsor may be deemed the winner, or Sponsor may apply additional criteria in its sole discretion.

Potential winners are subject to verification of eligibility and compliance with these Official Rules.

Potential winners will be notified using the contact methods described on the Contest Page, which may include email, direct message, or other communication channels associated with the platform used to submit the entry or the information provided with the entry form.

Within the timeframe specified in the notification (or, if not specified, within a reasonable period determined by Sponsor), each potential winner may be required to:

  • Confirm eligibility and acceptance of the prize;

  • Sign and return a declaration or affidavit of eligibility and liability/publicity release, where permitted by law; and

  • Provide any additional information reasonably requested by Sponsor for prize fulfillment or tax reporting purposes.

If a potential winner does not respond in time, does not return required documentation, is found to be ineligible, or otherwise fails to comply with these Official Rules, Sponsor may, in its sole discretion, disqualify that potential winner and select an alternate winner or decline to award the prize.

If you wish to withdraw your Submission from the Contest after entry, you may:

  • Contact Sponsor through the support or contact information provided on its website or the Contest Page, including your name, artist name, and a link to or clear description of your Submission, and state that you wish to withdraw from The Drop Challenge.

Sponsor will make reasonable efforts to honor opt-out requests going forward. However, Sponsor may not be able to remove content already included in materials created prior to processing your request.
